Mission & Values

At EDGE, we turn "what if" into "what is." Our mission is to provide anyone — regardless of background or income — with free access to high-tech tools, hands-on learning, and dedicated one-on-one mentorship to bring their ideas to life. Your bank account will never limit your potential here.

  • Radical Access
    Free use of world-class technology
  • Personal Growth
    One-on-one mentorship for every maker
  • Digital Equity
    Leveling the playing field for our community
  • Hands-On Innovation
    Learning by doing, building, and creating
